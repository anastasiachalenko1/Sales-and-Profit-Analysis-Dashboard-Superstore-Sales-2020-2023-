# Sales-and-Profit-Analysis-Dashboard-Superstore-Sales-2020-2023-
Built an interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing 9,000+ Superstore transactions across sales trends, profitability by sub-category, and U.S. delivery performance. Used dual-axis charts, calculated fields, color scales, and unified filters to surface margin risks and operational insights.

Tools: Tableau Public, Superstore Sales Dataset, Data Visualization, Dashboard Interactivity

Overview: Developed an interactive Tableau dashboard to analyze sales trends, profitability, and delivery efficiency across the U.S. The project focused on time series analysis, category performance, and delivery time mapping using BI techniques. The dashboard was designed as a business intelligence tool for operational teams to evaluate performance and identify process improvement opportunities.

Responsibilities: Built monthly sales and profit time series with dual-axis charts and filters for Ship Mode and Segment. Calculated relative profit by sub-category and shipping type using calculated fields and colour scales. Analyzed delivery times, visualized results on a U.S. map, and combined all visuals into a single interactive dashboard with unified filters.

Result: Translated operational and profitability business questions into structured visual analytics models, delivering an interactive, data-driven Tableau dashboard. Analyzed 9,000+ transactions to identify the top 20% product categories generating 63% of total profit. Identified negative margin exposure within specific subcategories (up to -12%), highlighting revenue leakage risk.
